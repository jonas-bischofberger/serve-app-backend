Introduction

The team
- consists of six to twelve players 
- Six players in the starting line-up = starting players 
- Each player takes a certain position at the beginning.

The playing field 
- 18x9m 
- There are attack lines (parallel to the center line) at a distance of three meters from the net/center line. 
- In the attacking zone (between the center line and the attacking lines), the ball may only be played over the net by the front players above the edge of the net.
- In the front zone, the ball may only be played over the net by back players below the edge of the net, or the ball may be hit behind the attack line after jumping off. However, the landing is allowed in the front zone.
